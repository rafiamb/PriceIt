## Problem Statement
Airbnb hosts often struggle to determine the **optimal nightly price** for their listings.  
Listing too high can result in fewer bookings, while listing too low reduces potential revenue.  

PriceIt predicts a competitive nightly rate for Airbnb properties based on **location, amenities, property features, and historical data**, helping hosts maximize revenue and stay competitive.
